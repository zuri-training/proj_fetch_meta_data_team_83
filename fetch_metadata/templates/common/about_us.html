Welcome To MetaTrack

Who We Are

MetaTrack is an online metadata extraction tool for files ranging from PDF, JSON, JPEG, amongst others. We are also a security and data protection website against embedded malware threats and virus attacks.

What We Do?

Think of Metadata as bits of information stored, hidden inside files, ranging from size to modification history and software tools used to create them.

MetaTrack extracts those needed informations on reuqest and allows you access to Export as a File or Share through a link. And that's not even all, protect your files against virus attacks and malware threats with our Security Protocols.
